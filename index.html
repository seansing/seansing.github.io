Prequel

Hi there, my name is Sean Sing Zhen Hau.

Intrigued by the hopes of decentralizating and fully connecting the world in a trustless manner, I found myself immersed in the depths of the promising technology that will soon bring this idea to life.

As I embarked on a journey to learn how it all worked and more importantly how I could contribute, I fell into a routine of spending many of my nights dabbling in Solidity smart contracts, snacking on intricately drizzled ganache Truffles, petting a fox named MetaMask and tabbing through the overflowing stacks in search of the JavaScript pieces to complete the big puzzle of Web3.

While I admit that it has not been easy trying to understand all the intricacies of the web and deep tech, I quickly learnt that with a whole lot of persistance, perseverance, nights with lofi beats and a bit of luck, anything is really possible.
